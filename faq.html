Frequently Asked Questions

Let's find quick answers to common questions.

How can I order food?

Simply open the app, browse through the menu, select your items, and proceed to checkout. Your delicious meal will be on its way!

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges are calculated based on the distance from the restaurant to your delivery address. The fee will be displayed at checkout.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is placed, you can track its status in the app from preparation to delivery.
